I'm an international student from Hong Kong and have been living in British Columbia for 7 years.
Having spent my first 3 years in Victoria, BC, I decided to enter the big city - the beautiful Vancouver. This has been a great journey so far and I hope to establish a career in Canada. Currently in my 4th year in SFU, I have developed great interests in game development and web development.

Projects I have worked on

Escape Dimension

A 3D Unity game Tyson Bulmer and I created for BC Game Jam 2017. Designed with minimal UI in mind, player has to look for clues and game mechanics by exploring and deciphering cryptic messages. Click here to play!

OpenCode

Our submission to nwHacks 2017, Opencode is a hub for beginner developers, where contributing to open-source projects only requires 2 steps to set up. This is my first using React and it certainly made a very educational experience.

MeetUpVancouver

An Android mobile app we developed for SFU "Google API Challenge" 24 Hour Hackathon. Users can host public events in the interactive map and save the details on Google Calendar and others can see what events are being held around them in Vancouver, and if they choose to participate, they can save the event to Google Calendar.

FoodNatic

FoodNatic is a webapp I developed in a group of 5 in Ruby on Rails. Hopefully you can find great inspiration for your new recipes here!
